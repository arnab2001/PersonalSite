## Overview

BLÅHAJ's Den is an educational website serving as a Wikipedia for sharks, providing comprehensive information about different shark species.

## Key Features

- **Shark Database**: Extensive information about various shark species
- **Dynamic Content**: PHP-powered dynamic content delivery
- **MySQL Storage**: Structured data storage for shark information
- **Educational Resource**: Detailed facts and information for shark enthusiasts
